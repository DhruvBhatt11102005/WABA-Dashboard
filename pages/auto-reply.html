Best Practices

Keep it concise
WhatsApp users prefer short, direct messages. Get straight to the point in your auto-replies.
Use broad keywords
Add multiple variations (e.g., 'hi', 'hello', 'hey') to ensure your rule catches common greetings.
Set expectations
If a human will follow up, let the user know approximately when they can expect a reply.
